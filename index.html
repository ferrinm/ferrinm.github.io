brainstorming organization of story

background and motivation
  • experimental measurements show wide variety of biophysical parameters among type 1 myosins
    • binding kinetics
    • off-rate force sensitivity
    • duty ratio
    • ATPase cycle kinetics
    • others?
  • myosins with different parameters have different biological functions
  • we don't know why the yeast myo5 would be good for CME, or whether the mammallian endocytic myosin has similar properties
question
  • more broad: what biophysical parameters of myosin are most important for CME robustness?
  • more specific: what is the mechanistic role of myo-actin force sensitivity in CME?
  • even more specific: what do we predict the parameters of mammalian endocytic myosin to be based on what works best in simulations
